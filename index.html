Research

My research goal is to build general-purpose humanoid robots by combining world-model-based prediction with action planning, enabling autonomous decision-making and robust generalization in real-world environments.

I previously worked on NLP, including prompt optimization for large language models, and I am now focusing on how learning and planning can be integrated to construct reliable decision-making agents for robotics.
